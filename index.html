Our Story

We started with a simple idea: that great coffee should never be complicated. Born from a shared love of slow mornings and the rich aroma of fresh grounds, The Artisan Bean is dedicated to the craft. Our small team sources ethically traded beans and roasts them in micro-batches right here, ensuring a rich, smooth flavor in every cup. It's an artisanal approach, rooted in tradition, that brings you simple, perfect pleasure.

Signature Drinks

Latte

Silky espresso blended with lightly steamed milk, finished with a subtle hint of house-made vanilla bean syrup

Cold Brew

Our signature dark roast, steeped for 18 hours to achieve maximum smoothness and a naturally sweet, low-acidity finish

Mocha
